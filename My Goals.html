My Goals and Ambitions

My Goals for 2017

Within the next year I hope to start my own business. Reselling is a pretty popular business as of late. If people get a chance to
purchase a limited shoe(a popular one being the Adidas Yeezy) they can choose to sell it to a population known as sneakerheads.
Sneakerheads are people who just really enjoy collecting shoes, and are very active in the sneaker community. There are many in the world, and
most of them enjoy having more than one pair of specific types of shoes. Reselling just one pair of limited sneakers can produce
thousands of dollars in profit.

I want to start an online retail store that people can list their products to sell to other interested people. Products
include: Limited shoes, limited apparel, as well as my own companies apparel. I strive to be a resource for people that helps them connect
with other people, and sell their products through a trusted company, as well as help people get the most money for their products.
Different websites in the business take up to 20% of the sale, including Ebay, and I think that is really unfair for the customers.
Along with this, sneaker customizations are pretty popular. I would to think of myself as a pretty creative and artsy
person, so I think that I can incorporate that into my business. This way, I am not targeting just the percentage of
people who will buy expensive sneakers that the majority view as "overpriced".

Like most fads, such as sneaker and limited clothes collecting, it will eventually end. So this company is temporary, and a short term goal for me.
It is also a way for me to gain experience in the retail and reselling world, as well as experience running my own small company. I'll use this experience
to decide whether or not I will continue to pursue a career in retail, or as a business owner.
