Hobbies and Activities

Snowboarding and Wakeboarding

One of my favorite things to do that I have loved since I was a child, is snowboarding. My oldest brother taught me how to snowboard at the age of three,
so I've been snowboarding for a total of 16 consecutive years now. A dream of mine is to snowboard all over the globe, from the Rockies of America, to
Japan's top resorts. Once winter ended, I was always so bored because I never had anything to do. I didn't like to skateboard like a lot of other snowboarders
do, so I turned to wakeboarding. It was just like snowboarding, and it hurts even less if you fall. I've only been wakeboarding for a short amount of time, but
I look forward to wakeboarding almost every day this coming summer at my new home that is next to a lake.

Sneaker Collecting

A large part of my motivation to start my business is that I'm really into shoes. Once I have a steady income, I will actually
start buying and collecting them, but right now I like to research rarity, as well as how to tell a fake shoe from a real shoe. As ridiculous as it sounds, many foreign
companies produce replica famous and expensive shoes. One of the most popular shoes they replicate is called the Adidas Yeezy by Kanye West. To give an idea as to how
limited it is, a pair was released worldwide on February 25th, and it sold out within 30 minutes. There were only 5400 pairs available throughout all of North America,
and it retailed at $220. Now people who were lucky enough to get a pair have been reselling them for over $2000. It's an absolutely insane market, and I can't wait to
dip my foot in it.

Working on Cars

Another hobby of mine is working on my car. I became really interested in cars and how they work when I was around 14. That was when I entered the market looking for
my own car to buy once I turned 16. My first car was a 2007 Subaru WRX. It's a type of car that comes with a turbo in it, and has a lot of options to help performance under
the hood, as well as on the exterior. Long story short, I painfully ended up selling it, and bought a 2012 Scion tC, which I still have today. This has been a much easier car
to work on, and has helped expand my general knowledge around cars. I've installed an aftermarket short ram intake which slightly improves performance as well as gas mileage.
My plans for this car is to install miscellaneous led lights which include fog lights as well as aftermarket speakers and a subwoofer, this will teach me more about the
electrical side of cars. Then I will put aftermarket rims on which along with making the car look goos, it reduces weight. Then I will install an aftermarket exhaust which
goes hand in hand with the intake and will help performance. A huge life goal of mine is to own my own car lift in my personal garage to make it easier to work on me and my
family's vehicles.
